About Us

Urban Hardwood Floors

Urban Hardwood floors is a hands-on, owner operated company. Urban Hardwood Floors is a unique flooring company and the only flooring professional you'll have to deal with, so you can trust us to handle your project with a high level of personal attention and dedication to quality which your project deserves. We are passionate about hardwood flooring and possess over 20 years of experience. We have worked on refinishing floors as old as 132 years made of fir tree wood and the largest install we've performed is a 3700 ft. border feature strip. Whether we are working on a centuries old wooden floor, or installing brand new flooring in a house which has never been occupied, we bring the same high level of enthusiasm to any project.
